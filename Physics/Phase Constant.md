The phase constant is a function of the frequency and the material.
$$\beta=k=\frac{\omega}{v}$$
Where:
- $\omega$ is the [[Angular Frequency]]
- $v$ is the [[Phase Velocity]]

# Wavenumber in free space

$$k_0 = \frac{\omega}{c}$$

Where:
- $c$ is the [[Speed of Light]]
